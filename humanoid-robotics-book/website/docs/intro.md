5. **The Autonomous Humanoid (Capstone Project)**: Complete integration of all concepts into a working system

### Why Physical AI Matters

Physical AI represents the convergence of artificial intelligence and physical systems, creating robots that can perceive, reason, and act in the real world. This field is crucial for:

- **Automation**: Enabling robots to perform complex tasks in unstructured environments
- **Human-Robot Collaboration**: Creating safe, intuitive interfaces between humans and machines
- **Research & Development**: Advancing our understanding of embodied intelligence
- **Industrial Applications**: Transforming manufacturing, logistics, and service industries
